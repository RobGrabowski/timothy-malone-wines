We are a small family owned and operated winery in the Willamette Valley, Oregon striving to make Pinot Noir that is reflective of this beautiful place we call home.

We engage in minimal-intervention winemaking practices inspired by our Oregon forefathers that result in authentic wines that are reflective of a time and place.

We hope that you find our wines engaging and worthy of your dinner table.

Vinyards

We currently source fruit from La Colina & La Sierra Vineyards of the Dundee Hills, Medici & Lia’s Vineyards of the Chehalem Mountains, Cancilla Vineyard of the Willamette Valley, and Garnier Vineyard of the Columbia Gorge.

La Colina is a beautiful Jory soil based site in southwestern Dundee, Oregon. Vines at this site were planted in 1999-2000. We currently are working with Wadenswil and Pommard clones planted on 3309 rootstock from blocks 10,12,13 & 14. La Colina is farmed and managed by George Hillberry.

Lia’s Vineyard is an extraordinary site around the Bald Peak section of Chehalem Mountain with a southern slope that ranges in elevation from 380-560ft. The soils are a mixture of Jory and Sedimentary and are home to a slew of interesting clones. We are currently working with 115, 777 & 828 clones of Pinot Noir from this vineyard. Lia’s vineyard is owned and managed by Todd Hansen and named after his cute cheerful daughter Lia.
