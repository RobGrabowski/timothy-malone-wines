We are a small family owned and operated winery in the Willamette Valley, Oregon striving to make Pinot Noir that is reflective of this beautiful place we call home.

We engage in minimal-intervention winemaking practices inspired by our Oregon forefathers that result in authentic wines that are reflective of a time and place.

We hope that you find our wines engaging and worthy of your dinner table.
